Powershame

Work like someone's watching

You sit down. You're determined to get stuff done. Today's going to be the day...
...until it isn't. You just can't seem to do the things you know you know you want to do. A few minutes on Facebook, a Youtube video or two...and there goes your ambitious project.

Powershame keeps you on track using the oldest, most powerful motivational tool on the world: Pride. Knowing that your friends will see what you're doing right now, those cat GIFs look a lot less appealing.

It's also a great way to keep track of your own work. Send yourself your own Powershame sessions and at the end of the day you can bask in pride as all your hard work is played back to you.

I created Powershame to help myself get things done. It's been incredibly effective, and if you're anything like me, you should give it a try.
